Product Description

SoundWave Pro X7 headphones represent the pinnacle of audio engineering, designed for audiophiles and music professionals who demand exceptional sound quality and comfort.

The Pro X7 features custom-tuned 40mm dynamic drivers that deliver rich, detailed sound across the entire frequency spectrum. The advanced active noise cancellation technology uses four microphones to continuously monitor and adapt to your environment, effectively blocking out unwanted noise.

Built with premium materials including memory foam ear cushions and a lightweight, adjustable headband, the Pro X7 ensures hours of comfortable listening. The foldable design and included premium travel case make these headphones the perfect companion for travelers and commuters.

With Bluetooth 5.2 technology, the Pro X7 provides a stable wireless connection with a range of up to 10 meters. For ultimate sound quality, you can also use the included audio cable for a wired connection. The built-in high-capacity battery delivers up to 40 hours of playback time, and fast charging gives you 5 hours of playback with just 10 minutes of charging.
